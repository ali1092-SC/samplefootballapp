TOURNAMENT TIMELINE

ROAD TO GLORY

June 11, 2026

Opening Match

Estadio Azteca, Mexico City — the world holds its breath as the tournament kicks off before 87,000 fans in one of football's most iconic arenas.

June 11 – July 2, 2026

Group Stage

48 nations, 12 groups, 64 matches — the most expansive group stage in World Cup history begins, with action across 16 cities in three countries.

July 4–9, 2026

Round of 32

The knockout rounds begin. For the first time in World Cup history, 32 teams compete in a preliminary knockout round, reducing the field to the Round of 16.

July 11–14, 2026

Round of 16 & Quarter-Finals

Sixteen nations remain. The quarter-finals separate the contenders from the pretenders, as the world's best clash in high-stakes knockout football.

July 18–19, 2026

Semi-Finals

New York / New Jersey and Los Angeles host the last four standing. Two matches. Four nations. One dream — to play in the final at MetLife Stadium.

July 22, 2026

Third Place Play-off

Two nations denied a place in the final compete for bronze and a final chance to savour the World Cup stage on the grandest platform in world football.

July 23, 2026 🏆

THE FINAL

MetLife Stadium, East Rutherford, New Jersey — 82,500 in attendance, five billion watching worldwide. The FIFA World Cup 2026 champion is crowned.
