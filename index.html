Tournament Timeline

Key milestones from the opening whistle to the Final.

June 11, 2026
🏟️ Opening Ceremony & First Match
The curtain rises at MetLife Stadium, New York/New Jersey. The USA kicks off the most-watched sporting event in history against a yet-to-be-confirmed opponent.
June 11 – July 2, 2026
⚽ Group Stage — 12 Groups
48 teams compete across 12 groups of 4. The top 2 from each group plus the 8 best third-placed teams qualify for the Round of 32.
July 4 – July 9, 2026
🏆 Round of 32 (First Knockout Round)
For the first time in World Cup history, 32 teams enter the knockout round — an unprecedented expansion of drama and upsets.
July 10 – July 13, 2026
⚡ Round of 16
16 survivors battle for a quarter-final place. This is where legends are made and fairy-tales end.
July 14 – July 15, 2026
🔥 Quarter-Finals
The elite 8 clash across prime stadiums in all three host nations, bringing the tournament to its most electric phase.
July 17 – July 18, 2026
🌟 Semi-Finals
Four teams, two matches. The world watches to see who will contest football's ultimate prize on July 19.
July 19, 2026
🏅 The Final — MetLife Stadium
Two nations. One trophy. One moment of glory. The FIFA World Cup 2026 Final at MetLife Stadium, East Rutherford, New Jersey — the greatest match on Earth.
